About WatchPets Project

Watchpets is an application that helps you find your lost pet with the help of your close facebook friends, the most remarkable thing about our application is the simple way to send an alert to your close facebook friends with the detailed information of your lost pet, and the point where you had lost it.


The idea of creating this application was because weeks before a friend's pet was lost and to make known his loss he had to use his social networks, in which he did not give many characteristics of the pets and exact specifications of where he had lost the pet and that made his friends could not help him because they did not have all the information they required at first, so we had the idea of creating this application to solve this problem.
